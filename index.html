Meet the Gang!

Scooby-Doo

Always hungry, and is easily scared. You can get him to do anything for some Scooby snacks.

Shaggy Rogers

Shaggy has the same personality as Scooby. It makes sense, since Scooby is his dog/best friend. Like human, like pet.

Velma Dinkley

Velma is the brains of the gang. She is very smart and is always loosing her glasses.

Daphne Blake

Daphne is the "Pretty" girl of the gang. She's very stylish and loves purple. She's also known for always getting captured by the villians. Her and Fred have a romance as well.

Fred Jones

Fred is the leader of the gang and driver of the Mystery Machine Has a romance thing with Daphne.

The Mystery Machine

The gang's transportation.
